What Is Special Needs Pediatric Dentistry?

Special needs pediatric dentistry focuses on treating children with developmental, physical, behavioral, sensory, or cognitive conditions that require a customized approach to dental care. This includes children with autism, ADHD, Down syndrome, cerebral palsy, or medical conditions that make dental visits challenging. Our team takes the time to understand your child’s needs, explain every step, and ensure a calm and positive experience.

Why Choose Special Needs Pediatric Dentistry in Corona

Choosing a dental office experienced in special needs care makes all the difference. Our approach includes:

  • Extra Time and Patience – Longer appointments so your child never feels rushed.
  • Kid-Friendly Communication – Simple language and visual aids to explain steps.
  • Gentle Behavior Management – Desensitization and positive reinforcement to reduce anxiety.
  • Sedation Options – Safe pediatric sedation for children who need additional support.
  • Parent Involvement – Parents are encouraged to stay with their child for reassurance.

Our Approach to Special Needs Dental Care

Tips for Parents Preparing for a Dental Visit

Schedule morning appointments when your child is calm, bring a favorite toy or blanket, and practice “pretend visits” at home. Share important details about triggers or preferences so we can adjust the visit. Most importantly, stay positive — your calm presence helps your child feel secure.
